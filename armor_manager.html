How to Use the Armor Manager

  • Filter & Group: Use the dropdowns below to filter by Class and group by Armor Slot or Set.
  • Prioritize Archetypes: Drag and drop the archetypes within each pane to set your preference (top is highest priority).
  • Keep or Junk: Check the box next to an archetype to keep it, or uncheck to automatically mark it as junk.
  • Keep Logic: Check "Keep at least 1 per Name" to ensure the absolute best roll is kept even if all its archetypes are unchecked.
  • Process: Click "Process Armor" to generate a new CSV with "keep" and "junk" tags applied!
